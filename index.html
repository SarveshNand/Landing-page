Spider-Man is awesome

Spider-Man is one of the most iconic superheroes of all time — created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko, he first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 (1962). His real name is Peter Parker, a high school (later college) student from Queens, New York, who gains spider-like powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider.

Some random information of Spidey

Spider-Man webbing dissolves in about an hour. — Peter made sure his web formula biodegrades so he doesn’t leave sticky messes all over New York.

Spider-Man was the first teenage superhero in comics who wasn’t a sidekick — this was revolutionary for 1962.

Peter Parker works as a freelance photographer for the Daily Bugle, run by J. Jonah Jameson — who famously hates Spider-Man but loves selling photos of him.

The Spider-Man suit has had over 60 variations, including the Iron Spider armor from Tony Stark and the Black Symbiote Suit (which later became Venom).

“With great power comes great responsibility.”

-Uncle Ben, Uncle of Peter Parker aka Spider-Man
